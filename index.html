Become an Astronaut

Training Includes:

Spacia provides proper training for our clients to become real astronauts. Participants train for two days a week for three months (at the minimum) to be eligible to go to space.

Don't worry, we won't let participants drive a space shuttle all alone. A special crew would be travelling alongside you for each experience. The crew would be responsible of taking off and landing of the shuttle. They would also be touring and taking care of your trip in space.

  • Diet control
  • Patterned exercise
  • Zero gravity experience
  • Survival tactics
  • Getting used to weightlessness

...and much more!
